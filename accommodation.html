    Accommodation
    Lotus Hotel Pang Suan Kaew  ( 4 stars from 5 by Agoda)



    Lotus Hotel Pang Suan Kaew is situated only 10 minutes from Chiang Mai international airport. The hotel is conveniently located in the center of many tourist attractions including Doi Suthep temple; the most famous temple in the city, Suan Dok ancient temple, Huay Kaew waterfall, Chiang Mai zoo and Kad Suan Kaew shopping complex.


    All rooms are decorated in a contemporary Lanna Style to enhance the Lanna Cultural experience. (Two persons per room)

    ROOM size: 40 square meters



    In room Amenities and Benefits

    • Complimentary bottled drinking water
    • Bathtub
    • Hair dryer
    • Connecting ROOM available upon request
    • WIFI Internet (Free)
    • Safe deposit box
    • Front Desk

    Food

    San Kam Pheang: offers international buffet breakfast 06:00 - 10:00 am.


    Hotel facilities


    For your enjoyable stay, Lotus Hotel Pang Suan Kaew provides you with many facilities.

    • Olympic Size Swimming Pool
    • Fitness
    • Sauna & Steam
    • Thai Traditional Massage
    • ROOM Service
    • Laundry Service
    • Kad Suan Kaew shopping complex (Open 11.00 – 21.00)
