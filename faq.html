Frequently Asked Question

Q: What is included in 100-dollar-a-person application fee?
A: The application fee includes:
  • Registration fee
  • Hotel (4 days and 4 nights)
  • All meals and snacks, including Khan-tok Dinner
  • Transportation fee in Chiang Mai (Exclude traveling expenses from your country or city, such as airline tickets, train tickets, etc.)
  • Entrance fee for tourist attractions
Q: What is the role of an advisor in this competition?
A: The role of an advisor is to take care of the students. Besides, we provide many interesting activities for advisors also.
Q: Who can be the advisor of a team?
A: It can be any advisor in the faculty, a lecturer or a professor, who can take care of the students considering both academic and other.
Q: Can we register the CMU-IMC without an advisor?
A: It is not compulsory for the advisor positon. However, we encourage each team to have an advisor coming along with the participants.
Q: If we are not ready to provide some pieces of information at the time of registration, e.g. passport numbers or the team photo, can we send them later?
A: Yes, you can inform us this necessity and send those pieces of the information later via E-mail.
Q: Could you help arrange a flight or any transportation from our country/city?
A: We are sorry to inform you that we cannot do that because it is inappropriate to suggest the participants things similar to a commercial offer, according to Thai Medical Student’s Code of Conduct.
Q: For early arrival or late departure, will we able to stay in the hotel for more nights?
A: Yes, please inform us and we will arrange it for you. However, you have to pay for the additional cost.
