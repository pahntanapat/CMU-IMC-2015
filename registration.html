Applicant qualifications

Applicants must be enrolled and registered in a medical undergraduate degree program at the time of application and competition.

  • Certificate of student state or a copy of student ID card required.
  • Each university/college can send up to 3 teams of students. ONE team consists of 4 medical students with or without 1 professor or advisor. (Notice: the professor can be the same person for all 3 teams)
  • Please note that participants who have joined the Chiang Mai University International Medical Challenge (CMU-IMC) 2014 can re-attend.

Registration system will be opened on 1st March 2015

Application Fee

100 USD per person (500 USD per team including 1 professor or advisor and 4 medical students)
* For Thai applicants, the application fee is 3,200 Baht per person. (16,000 Baht per team including 1 professor or advisor and 4 medical students)

What is included in 100-dollar-a-person application fee?

  • Registration fee
  • Hotel (4 days and 4 nights)
  • All meals and snacks, including Khan-tok Dinner
  • Transportation fee in Chiang Mai (Exclude traveling expenses from your country or city, such as airline tickets, train tickets, etc.)
  • Entrance fee for tourist attractions

  1. Access to CMU-IMC website: http://cmu-imc.med.cmu.ac.th
  2. Go to ‘Registration’ section.
  3. Complete the application form provided. The fields required are marked with (*).
  4. Submit your information by clicking ‘Register’ button.
  5. Transfer the application fee using the information below.

  6. Log in to 'Registration status" section on http://cmu-imc.med.cmu.ac.th to submit your transfer slip.
  7. Upload your team picture to "Registration status" section on http://cmu-imc.med.cmu.ac.th. The picture will be used to introduce your team on Facebook page.
