First Round

  • Multiple choice questions (5 choices)
  • 80 questions – each is worth 1 point
  • Test duration: 120 minutes
  • 12 teams with the highest scores will pass to the semi-final round.

Semi-Final Round

  • Teams will be arranged into 2 divisions
  • Competition format: Short answer question; written answer.
  • There will be 11 questions, divided into 3 levels
      • - Easy questions: 4 questions – each is worth 5 points
      - Moderate questions: 3 questions – each is worth 10 points
      - Difficult questions: 4 questions – each is worth 15 points
  • 2 teams with the highest scores from each division will pass to the final round.

Revival Round

  • Teams that are eliminated from the semi-final round will have another chance in the revival round.
  • Competition format: Short answer question; written answer.
  • All teams will start with 35 points. For an incorrect answer, points will be deducted as the following rules
      • - Easy question: loses 5 points
      - Moderate question: loses 10 points
      - Difficult question: loses 15 points
           *A correct answer does not value any points
  • Teams which points decrease to 0 will be eliminated immediately.
  • The last team remaining will continue to the final round.

Final Round

  • The competition will be separated into 4 parts

    • Part 1
    • Competition format: Short answer question; written answer
    • 8 questions – each is worth 20 points
    • Each team must send 1 member as a participant to answer the question.
    • Each member will have to answer a total of 2 questions, so in the team, all must take turns in participating, one by one.
    Part 2
    • Competition format: Short answer question; Oral answer
    • 5 questions – each item is worth 30 points
    • Teams must give an answer with supported information
    • Clues will be provided, though opening one clue will deduct 5 points from the points of the question (30 points)
    Part 3
    • Competition format: Short answer question; Oral answer within 15 seconds
    • 4 questions, scoring due to the answer given
    • Each team is to send a pair of members to answer the question. The other pair is to participate in the next question. This alternated participation will continue on until all items are completed.
    Part 4
    • Competition format: spot diagnosis; oral answer
    • 3 questions - each item is worth 60 points
    • All five teams are to open parts of the picture clue. Any team who knows the diagnosis of the lesion (on the picture) can give an answer within a limited time, though if the answer is incorrect, the team will lose points.
    • Team with the highest total-score will become the winner of CMU-IMC 2015.

    * The rules could be changed if considered appropriate.
