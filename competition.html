The Competition





First Round

  • Multiple choice questions (5 choices)
  • 80 items - 1 point each
  • Test duration: 120 minutes
  • The top 12 highest total-scoring teams pass to the Semi-Final round.

Semi-Final Round

  • Stage competition (Compete on stage)
  • Teams will be divided into 2 divisions.
  • Short answer questions
  • Write down the answer on the objects provided
  • 11 items
      • - Easy: 4 items - 5 points each
      - Medium: 3 items - 10 points each
      - Hard: 4 items - 15 points each
  • Can use a helper/hint “Take a risk” for 2 times.
    • “Take a Risk"
    When you use this item, it will give your team a chance to get (or lose) more points as listed below
      - right answer: got 10 points from easy questions, 20 for medium questions and 30 for hard questions
      - wrong answer: reduce the points from easy questions for 5, Medium difficulty questions for 10 and Hard questions for 15
  • 2 Teams with the highest total score(from 2 divisions – total of 4 teams) will pass to the final round

Revival Round

  • Teams that are eliminated from the semi-final round will have another chance in the revival round.
  • Stage competition (Compete on stage)
  • Short answer questions
  • Write down the answer on the objects provided
  • Starting with 35 points, wrong answering will reduce the score.
      • - Easy: reduce 5 points
      - Medium: reduce 10 points
      - Hard: reduce 15 points
  • Can use a helper/hint “Power plus” for 1 time
    • Power Plus
    When the item is used, the point criteria changes as listed below
      - Right answer: got 10 points from easy questions, 20 for medium questions and 30 for hard questions
      - Wrong answer: reduce the points from easy questions for 5, Medium difficulty questions for 10 and Hard questions for 15
  • 2 Teams with the highest total score(from 2 divisions – total of 4 teams) will pass to the final round
  • Any team scoring zero will be eliminated immediately.
  • The remaining teams pass to the final round

Final Round

  • Stage competition (Compete on stage)
  • Short answer questions
  • Divided into 4 parts

    • Part 1
      Oral answer
      4 items - 10 points each
      15 seconds per each item
    Part 2
      Oral answer
      5 items - 30 points each
      Answer with the information provided by opening clue pictures
      Opening more clue pictures reduces 5 points per time
    Part 3
      Oral answer
      4 items right answer: plus 30 points per item, wrong answer: reduce 20 points per item
      15 seconds per item
    Part 4
      Write down the answer on the objects provided
      3 items, 60 points per item
  • Team with the highest total score of all 4 parts will become a winner of CMU-IMC 2015.

* The rules could be changed if considered appropriate.
