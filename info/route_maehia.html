Wat Ton Kain

Wat Ton Kain- was one of the exotic and unique temple in Chiang Mai. The temple was built in the northern Thai tradition called ‘Lanna’. The temple is highlighted by The main hall which was beautifully decorated by typical Lanna woodcarvings. There is also Chaturamook hall which is the only one of this type in the northern.

Royal Flora Ratchaphruek

Royal Flora Ratchaphruek- is an outdoor flower exhibition. More than 2,200 species of plants are exhibited here which makes it one of the most visiting places in Chiang Mai. Not only many species of topical trees and flowers are exhibited here, but there are also the exhibition of New theory Agricultural, insects worlds and also indoor flower exhibitions. The exhibition is highlighted by Ho Kham Royal Pavilion which was built in honor of King Bhumibol and his delication.

Chiangmai night safari

Chiangmai night safari is the nocturnal zoo which was divided to many interesting zone. Jaguar trail or walking zone is the walking trail which can experience many species of closely and safe enhancing with beautiful environment. Savanna Safari is exhibition mostly for the Herbivore animals. Predator Prowl is the carnivorous animal zone. Besides animal zone, there are also walking tracts in which you can explore the wonderful of topical plants and flowers.
