What is CMU-IMC?

CMU-IMC stands for "Chiang Mai University International Medical Challenge". It is an international competition held annually by Chiang Mai University Faculty of Medicine, Thailand, attended by medical students from various countries around the world.
This year’s theme is “Basic to Clinical Biochemistry”

What will you get ?
Schedule

What will you get ?

  • Experience our challenging examination and quiz show in the field of medical knowledge.
  • Opportunity to expand your knowledge with other students by exchanging information and attitudes about medical issues.
  • Enhance your global network and friendship with friends from various countries.
  • Visit Chiang Mai’s tourist attractions you should not miss with our guides. After enjoying the trip, be fascinated by our special Lanna cultural dinner, known as “Kan-Tok”.
